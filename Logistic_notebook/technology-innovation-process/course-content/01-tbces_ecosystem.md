### üß† Theoretical Content

#### 1. What is a Technology-Based Company (TBC)?

A TBC is an organization whose core business model revolves around exploiting scientific and technological capabilities. They transform research and development (R\&D) results into innovative products, processes, or services with high value-added.

```mermaid
mindmap
  root((TBC / EBT))
    High Tech Focus
      Intensive R&D
      Innovative Products
    Scalability
      Global Reach
      Rapid Growth
    Knowledge Base
      Highly Skilled Team
      Patents & IP
```

#### 3. Technology Readiness Level (TRL)

The TRL scale (developed by NASA) measures the maturity of a technology from ideological concept to market-ready product.

```mermaid
flowchart TD
    subgraph "Phase 1: Research (TRL 1-3)"
        T1["TRL 1: Basic principles observed"]
        T2["TRL 2: Technology concept formulated"]
        T3["TRL 3: Experimental proof of concept"]
    end

    subgraph "Phase 2: Development (TRL 4-6)"
        T4["TRL 4: Tech validated in lab"]
        T5["TRL 5: Tech validated in relevant environment"]
        T6["TRL 6: Prototype demonstrated in relevant environment"]
    end

    subgraph "Phase 3: Deployment (TRL 7-9)"
        T7["TRL 7: System prototype demo in operational environment"]
        T8["TRL 8: System complete and qualified"]
        T9["TRL 9: Actual system proven in operational environment"]
    end

    T1 --> T2 --> T3 --> T4 --> T5 --> T6 --> T7 --> T8 --> T9
```
