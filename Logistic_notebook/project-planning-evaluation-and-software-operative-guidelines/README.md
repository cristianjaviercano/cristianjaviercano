### üéØ Course Objectives

By the end of this module, you will be able to:

1. **Identify Opportunities:** Analyze market failures and business opportunities to generate viable investment alternatives.
2. **Structure Projects:** Develop comprehensive Market, Technical, Administrative, Legal, and Financial studies.
3. **Evaluate Viability:** Determine financial feasibility using rigorous indicators such as NPV (Net Present Value) and IRR (Internal Rate of Return).
4. **Manage Strategy:** Understand the project lifecycle and apply strategic management principles during execution.

### üõ†Ô∏è Tools & Methodology

This course uses a **"Learning by Doing"** approach. While we cover the theoretical frameworks required by the syllabus, we will also leverage technology to modernize the formulation process.

* **Theory:** Based on standard methodologies (Sapag Chain, PMI, MGA).
* **Documentation:** All lecture notes, slides, and guides are hosted here on **GitBook**.
* **Computation:** We will use **Python (Jupyter Notebooks)** for:
  * Market forecasting (Time series analysis).
  * Financial sensitivity analysis.
  * Decision matrixes for technical location.
